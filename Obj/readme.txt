High Hedges
---

These are 12 unit high hedges for Thief 2 (NewDark). There are 12, 16, 24 and 32 unit long varieties.

Model created by Nick Dablin (nicked).
Textures created from images from cgtextures.com

---

INSTALLATION

Place bin files in the /obj folder of your Thief installation.
Place tga files in the /obj/txt16 folder of your Thief installation.
